Recent History

A couple of years ago the state invested a lot into cryptocurrency and offered the crypto whales the opportunity to acquire residency if they invested part of their crypto inside the country. Not only did the government invest in this revolution in the financial system but they doubled downed on it when they declared Bitcoin as the second official currency with the US dollar. El Salvador is a vibrant country filled to the brim with culture and history. The people of this country truly do not let their history die. There is still to this day lot of influence from the colonial period and indigenous culture. The heroes and villains that roamed the land can still be found in monuments and murals around the cities and towns. The writers and poets that are admired can still be enjoyed sice their craft is preserved in every library or bookstore.

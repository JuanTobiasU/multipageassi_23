The magic of being everywhere

El Salvador is the smallest country in the American Continent, nonetheless, its small territory brings some magical charm to its landscapes, pre-Hispanic archeological sites and one the most sought-after beaches for surfing in the world. The thumb of the Americas as it is playfully called has a lot of things to do with a small package. It is not a slight exaggeration! You can sway the way you visit the country on the go, you want to visit the towns and enjoy great food and then you want to go to the beach and then round up your day by staying in a mountain house, everything in one day.

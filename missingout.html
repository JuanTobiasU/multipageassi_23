Dont miss out

Something you do not want to miss is the food. The most famous dish in El Salvador is “La Pupusa” which is a corn tortilla with filling; it can be cheese, beans, meat, chicken, pork, flowers, or all the above. Not only that, but there is also a zone near the international airport called “pupusodromo” which is a corridor full of restaurants that serve pupusas, so...pick your poison.
